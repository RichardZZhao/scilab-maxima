Purpose

Change "sign" of strings.

This is used to replace symbolic change of sign for intermediate calculus (symbolic multiplication calls Maxima, so, needs a lot of CPU time).


Synopsis
  • [cc] = strchsign(aa)


See also: strminus, stradd, strvconcat, strhconcat, strpow.
EXAMPLE
   strchsign('1/(c+b)')
   strchsign(['a+1','b';'1','2/a'])
